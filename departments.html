How We Accelerate Your Business Growth

IconiaTech works with you to fully understand the impact of improved data management & governance on each of your business units. We address the inter-dependencies, relationships & cultural nuances that can create unnecessary confusion within the organisation.

Executive & C-Suite

Whether you're on a flight, or relaxing at a sporting event, don’t let expired data spoil your day. As an executive, you are constantly seeking ways to improve your business, and you can benefit from real-time, fresh intelligence to make the right call. IconiaTech offers neat & insightful visualisation tools to cater your growth mindset.

Finance & Risk

From receivables to payables, audit to treasury, credit-control to profit & loss, IconiaTech will help you leverage data to improve your financial management, analysis, and reporting by employing dynamic & tabulated visualisations, alerts, charts & graphs.

HR & People

The Human Resources function has evolved into an involved & intuitive business process that requires dynamic interaction with the people that make your organisation tick. A happy workforce is ultimately a productive, and IconiaTech has the right solutions to help you get closer to your employees by leveraging your data.

Information & Communication

Help your IT department gain freedom to innovate & address prevailing technological challenges, by taking away the pressure of ad hoc requests & time-consuming tasks. IconiaTech works closely with your IT professionals to understand your technology landscape & strategy.

Inventory, Supply Chain & Logistics Management

Get a 360° perspective of your dead-stock, over-stock, stock-on-hand, lead-times, goods-in-transit, storage-costs, and customer-demand by leveraging the experience of IconiaTech’s Inventory Analytics solutions.

Production

Fine-tune the production process from raw materials sourcing, to packaging & labelling to accurately determine where to eliminate waste, and optimise resources.

Sales & Marketing Operations

Is your organisation seriously focused on growth & stability? Do you struggle to identify & incentivize your top talent? Are your campaigns failing to capture your outreach objectives? Allow IconiaTech’s solutions to reveal trends, opportunities, inefficiencies, and untapped resources by employing visualisation techniques that deliver up-to-date sales metrics to your fingertips.
