Home - Services

These are the services we offer.

Data Handling & Interpretation

Did you know that your data is talking even though you’re not listening? Do you know what your data can tell you? A data conversation is the most truthful conversation one can have. We help you clean, enrich model, and restructure your data.

ETL & Warehousing

We use our vast experience with top class tools and software to extract data from all your systems and then then securely place it in a central area. By doing this, we ensure that all your systems are speaking in the same language for maximum insights.

Visualization & Presentation

Various tools that allow for beautiful interactive dashboards, reports, alerts and self-service analysis.

Cloud Deployment

Working with the popular cloud platforms to enable smooth and elastic deployment of solutions.

Big Data

Tools, platforms and concepts that enable the customers to explore vast amounts of data (streaming data or batch data).

Machine Learning

Tools and algorithms that can learn the extracted data and provide for predictions and detection of patterns and trends.

Streaming

Do you have time sensitive data that you’d want real time analysis on? Our team will open up a world of ‘on the fly analytics’ that helps you make timely data driven decisions for maximum gain.

End Device Integration

Integrating to devices, e.g. Printers, Access Control Solutions, Sensors, Communications devices, etc. Then generating Dashboards, Reports and Analytics from these devices.
